### Detalle de integraciГіn de proveedores (auth В· endpoints В· transformaciГіn)
- **Travelpayouts / Aviasales (primario):** auth = header **`X-Access-Token: $TRAVELPAYOUTS_TOKEN`** (sin OAuth, sin Bearer).
