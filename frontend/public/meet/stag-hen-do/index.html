Planning a stag or hen do has a catch nobody mentions: the crew doesn't all live in the same place anymore. Some are back home, some in the capital, one or two abroad. So the usual mess starts: the destination one person suggests is dirt cheap for them and expensive for whoever flies from the other end. You end up picking by who shouts loudest in the group chat, not by what works for everyone.

The idea is to flip it: instead of fixing the destination and then checking flights, you drop in the city each person flies from and let the cheapest destination for the group fall out. And since on a stag do nobody wants to be the one paying triple, you can sort by fairest — the place where the gap between who pays most and least is smallest.

One more thing that helps with a big group: there's a link you share in the chat and each person adds their own city, so you're not guessing where everyone flies from or chasing people for their dates. Once the group is complete, out comes the destination that splits the cost best across everyone.

Frequently asked questions

Does it work for any group size?

Yes. The bigger and more scattered the group, the more it helps, because doing the total cost and a fair split by hand is impossible.

Do I need to sign up?

No. Put in the cities and the destination comes out; no account needed. There's an optional group link so each person can add their own.

Are the prices real?

They're an estimate from cached real searches, strongest in Europe. Check the live price from the tool before booking.
